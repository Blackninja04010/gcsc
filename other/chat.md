# Chat secrets and more

## Messages

### Emojis
- `:1:` -
- `:2:`, `:smile:`, `:)` -
- `:3:`, `:frown`, `:(`
  ...

### Chat Codes
- [bal], [balance] and [$] - player's balance
- [stars] and [s] - player's stars
- [KillLevel] and [Level.Kills] - player's kill level
- [TradeshopLevel] and [Level.Tradeshop] - player's tradeshop level
- [PlaytimeLevel] and [Level.Playtime] - player's playtime level
